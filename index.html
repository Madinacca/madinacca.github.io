About Us

Building Communities, Empowering Lives, Transforming the Future At Madinacca Builders, we don’t just build homes—we build legacies. Founded by visionary leader Asif Zia, we are dedicated to creating communities that reflect innovation, sustainability, and a commitment to making a positive impact on the world. With over 25 years of combined expertise, our team understands that real estate is more than just bricks and mortar. It’s about crafting spaces where families can grow, businesses can thrive, and dreams can flourish. Every project we undertake is guided by our core principles of quality, affordability, and social responsibility. Our Mission We aim to redefine the real estate industry by providing: • Luxury living at competitive prices, offering larger square footage and premium finishes to cater to the evolving needs of young families and professionals. • Sustainable housing solutions that prioritize eco-friendly designs and green building practices. • Community-focused initiatives that empower individuals and create long- term value. A Vision Rooted in Giving Back At the heart of Madinacca Builders lies a profound commitment to social responsibility. We believe in reinvesting our success into the communities we serve, ensuring a brighter future for those who need it most. Following transformative programs: 1. Orphanage Support Program We are committed to empowering orphaned children by providing 2.5% of our net profits dedicated to providing access to high-quality education that equips them to compete globally. Our program will: • Partner with local and international institutions to ensure children receive education that goes beyond basic schooling. • Provide mentorship opportunities and career guidance to help them excel in life. • Create nurturing environments that foster creativity, resilience, and leadership. 2. Mental Health Initiatives Understanding the growing challenges of mental health, we have pledged ongoing support to mental health programs in partnership with the cities of Ajax and Whitby. This initiative aims to: • Raise awareness about mental health issues. • Provide funding for counseling and wellness programs. • Build safe spaces for individuals seeking help, ensuring no one feels alone in their journey. 3. Community Support for New Immigrants Recognizing the struggles faced by new immigrants, we are developing community programs to: • Assist with housing, employment, and educational opportunities. • Offer workshops and mentorship programs to help them integrate successfully into Canadian society. • Create a support network that fosters inclusion and belonging. A Legacy of Excellence With each project, Madinacca Builders aims to leave a lasting legacy—not just in architecture but in the lives we touch. From supporting families to uplifting vulnerable communities, we are shaping a future where success is measured not only by profits but also by the difference we make..

Our Projects

Current Project: 527 Kingston Road West, Ajax

Madinacca Builders proudly presents 527 Kingston Road West, a landmark mixed-use development located in the heart of Ajax’s Village Core. Designed to cater to modern families and businesses, this 3-storey building combines luxury living and prime retail opportunities.

  • Residential Units: 20 spacious luxury condos with 1-bedroom, 2-bedroom, and 3-bedroom+den configurations.
  • Retail Space: 1,405 sq. ft. of prime ground-floor retail space.
  • Parking: Above-ground parking for 29 vehicles.
  • Accessibility: Two high-speed elevators for seamless convenience.

Luxury Features:

  • Quartz countertops and smooth ceilings.
  • Smart home technology for modern living.
  • Energy-efficient lighting and thoughtfully designed layouts.

Financial Overview:

  • Total Revenue: $16.4 million.
  • Estimated ROI: 25–30%.
  • Profitability: Gross profit of $5.3–$6.5 million.
